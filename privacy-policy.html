Zomes Development Privacy Policy

Last updated March 12, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Zomes Development, LLC(collectively, “Zomes Development”, “us” or “we”) collects, uses and protects your personal information. If you have any questions that this policy does not address, please contact us at support@Zomes Development.com

Zomes Development invests in real estate with a focus on properties we believe will be attractive short-term vacation rentals and suitable for third-party rental platforms such as Airbnb or Vrbo.

Information Collected by Zomes Development

In this Privacy Policy, we use the term “personal information” to refer to information that can be used by itself or in combination with other available information to identify or be associated with you. Directly identifying information includes, for example, information such as your name, e-mail address, and date of birth. Personal information that is associated or linked to you includes data related to your interaction and usage of our Website, App, services, offerings and products.

We collect personal information when you submit subscription documents to us, if you authorize Zomes Development to provide your information to us, or communicate and interact with us in other ways, online and offline.
We collect the information you submit when you:

  • Visit our websites or use the Zomes Development mobile application (the “App”)
  • Register to set up a Zomes Development account on the Website or the App
  • Complete and submit our Subscription Agreement and other Regulation A Offering materials either directly to us or through Zomes Development
  • Communicate with us in your capacity as an investor or potential investor

During our financing phases, we will collect personal information to effectuate our Regulation A Offering (“Investor Information”). Over time as our services, products and offerings change, the Investor Information we collect may change. At this time, when you register with Zomes Development on the Website or the App or by executing our Subscription Agreement, we collect the following Investor Information:

  • Your first name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Social Security Number
  • Residential Address
  • Taxpayer Identification Number
  • Name of a Joint Subscriber
  • Social Security Number of the Joint Subscriber
  • If relevant, information related to a custodial account
    • Type of account
    • EIN of account
    • Address of account provider

We also use cookies and similar internet technologies to automatically collect information as you use our Website and App. See Cookie Collection and E-mail Marketing.

Automated Online Data Collection

We (and our third-party service providers) may place cookies, web beacons, scripts, and similar tracking technologies and device identifiers on your web browser or mobile device when you visit our Website or App. (In this Privacy Policy we use the term “cookies” to refer generally to cookies, tracking technologies, and other device identifiers.) You can use the settings or tools offered on your browser to refuse or deny cookies, or to delete cookies that have already been stored. (See “Cookie Collection and E-mail Marketing” below.)

Log in and identification: To recognize and verify you and your device when you log in to access your account and use the Website or App by storing data including, for example, your password, device and browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP) and Internet Protocol (IP) addresses), device and browser identifiers and information (including device, application, or browser type, version, plug-in type and version, operating system, and other technical data).

Features and performance: To provide functionality for the features and options you select to use on our Website or App; deliver consistent performance; recognize and implement your preferences; and enable information sharing with third-party providers.

Location: If you visit our Website or App using a mobile device, we obtain geolocation data from device sensors, GPS signals, WiFi access points, and cell towers, unless you disable location information in your device setting. We can also more generally identify your location through IP address or mobile device identifier.

Data analytics: Track and log web usage data about your interactions with our Website and App and online activities including, for example, browsing history, usage rates, settings preferences, content interactions (including searches, page views, downloads, and prints), and user journey history (including clickstreams and page navigation, URLs, timestamps, content viewed or searched for, page interactions, and similar data).

Advertising: Cookies used for advertising purposes may be set through our Website or the App by us or our advertising partners. We and our advertising partners may use cookies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on our Website or App and on other websites and mobile applications. Advertising cookies do not store directly personal information, but store data that may uniquely identify your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will still receive advertisements, but they will be less targeted specifically to you.

Email Interconnectivity: If you receive email from us, we use internet tools to capture data related to when you open our message and click on any links or banners it contained. We use this information to track the effectiveness of our communications with you.

We may use the information we collect about you (including Investor Information, to the extent applicable) to effectuate our Regulation A Offering, including to:

  • Conduct our Regulation A Offering
  • To make our Regulation A Offering available to existing stockholders and prospective investors
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations related to our Regulation A Offering
  • To verify Investor Information
  • To communicate with you regarding our Regulation A Offering, including responding to comments, questions, and requests
  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Website, the App, our online investor accounts services
  • To customize and personalize certain features of the Website and App
  • To communicate with you regarding changes to the Website, the App or our Regulation A offering and our Fractional Interest Offering
  • Enable you to access and use the Website and the App and improve their functionality and performance
  • To provide customer service, and communicate with you for marketing purposes, including information about promotions and rewards offerings
  • To improve the technical and non-technical aspects of the website and the App
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with the Site for performance, marketing or advertising purposes
  • Protect our Site and investors by investigating suspicious activity and unauthorized access
  • Comply with valid legal process and respond to governmental requests

How Zomes Development Shares Your Information

Zomes Development does not sell your personal information to third parties or share personal information for monetary or other consideration. To assist us in providing our services and offerings, Zomes Development may share some of your personal information with third parties and your personal information may be accessible to third parties in connection with services they provide to us, including the following:

Third-Party Service Providers: We share information, including certain personal information, with our third-party service providers that we use to: process authentication and log-in information; provide hosting for and maintenance of our Website and App, e-mail marketing, solicit investors, scheduling meetings, backup, storage, payment processing, analytics and other services for us. These third-party service providers may have access to or process your personal information for the purpose of providing these services for us. We do not permit our third-party service providers to use the personal information that we share with them for their own commercial purposes.

Analytics: We may collect, and we may engage third-party analytics providers to collect, metrics and information regarding your use of the Website and App, including evaluating how visitors use our Website and App (“Usage Data”) to develop new features, improve existing features or inform sales and marketing strategies. When we process Usage Data, any personal information is anonymized

Advertising and Marketing: We may use third-party advertising companies to serve advertisements on our Website and App and on selected websites and mobile applications operated by others. These companies may set their own cookies or use similar web technologies to collect information about your online and mobile device activities over time and across different websites. These companies then may direct advertisements to you based on your interests as suggested by the history of your visits to Website, App and other websites or mobile applications.

Community Forums: The Website and App may offer publicly accessible blogs, community forums, comments sections, discussion forums, or other interactive features (“Interactive Areas”). You should be aware that any information that you post in an Interactive Area might be read, collected, and used by others who access it. To request removal of your personal information from an Interactive Area, contact us at support@Zomes Development.com. In some cases, we may not be able to remove your personal information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to do so and why.

Affiliates: We may share information, including personal information, with companies affiliated with Zomes Development, and they will use that information only for the purposes that are consistent with those described in this Privacy Policy.

Legal or Regulatory Purposes: We share information with governmental and regulatory entities as necessary to fulfill our legal obligations. For our Regulation A Offering and our Fractional Interest offering, we will disclose information to the Security Exchange Commission as required under applicable law.

Promotional and Reward Partners: We may share information, including personal information, to third-party service providers who provide promotional and reward items and offers associated with investing in the Regulation A Offering.
