Circle - Sedona

266 Circle Dr, Sedona, AZ 86336

Project Description

Show stopping Red Rock Vistas in all directions from this dramatic hillside lot just steps away from all the amenities in highly desirable Uptown Sedona.

Located in a prime Sedona neighborhood with no HOA, this location is close to forest trails, charming galleries and shops, and popular restaurants.

Investment Highlights:
  • Approx 1/3 acre with City sewer, natural gas, electricity and water available

  • Rare uptown vacant lot. Central and private.
  • Unimpeded views of Cathedral Rock, Snoopy, Thunder Mountain, Wilson Mountain and Elephant Rock
  • Guest home permitted, which allows for room to build two STR units.
  • No HOA which removes risk of STR limitations being imposed in the futue.
  • High-end neighborhood.
