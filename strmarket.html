Adding STRs to your real estate portfolio has many strategic advantages

Passive Income

Mature hosting services means little to no management headaches.

Stable Cash Flow

In contrast to commercial tenants, STR vacancies are measured in days, not months or years.

Strong Exit Story

STR properties appeal to both Investors and Residential buyers.

Low Margin Hospitality

AirBnBs perform similarly to hotels when it comes to revenue, but run at less than half their management costs.

Inflation protection

Home values and rents typically increase during times of inflation

Diversification

Low correlation to other real-estate asset classes

The Opportunity of Unique Stays

Demand for Unique AirBnb Stays is Outmatching Supply

Airbnb’s Q2 2022 earnings noted that nights booked for unique homes were up 80 percent compared to pre-pandemic levels.

Tiny houses (+173%), barns (+160%), domes (134%), and treehouses (+116%) also saw particularly strong interest.

Airbnb recently introduced a new category of properties titled ‘OMG’ which captures the most luxurious, unique, and exclusive accommodations listed on the site. These properties stand out from the rest with their next-level amenities, outstanding service, and high-end furnishings.(learn more)

In 2022 AirBnb launched a $10M OMG competition to choose the best 100 OMG concept homes, and fund their buildout.
